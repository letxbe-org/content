type: definition
title: Subgroup
---

Let $G$ be a [group](@group). A subset $H \subseteq G$ is a **subgroup** of $G$, notation $H \le G$, if the following properties are satisfied:

1. $H \neq \emptyset$.
2. For all $x, y \in H$, the product $xy \in H$.
3. For all $x \in H$, the inverse $x^{-1} \in H$.
